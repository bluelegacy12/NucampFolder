This is my sister Eve, she is a cool and down-to-earth feline friend-o-mine! She is also shy of new people and has very strict demands for attention from our owners at certain times of the day. She also has a dedicated spot in the bed at bedtime and she never misses it!

"Ash"

Introducing the largest member of our family, my brother Ash! Some say he looks unwelcoming from his face, but he's actually the friendliest and least shy of us all! He always wants to meet new people and treats them just like his new best friend. His tail is slightly shorter and fluffier than the rest of us, and he has no flexibility or bendability with his tail, so it always goes straight up or wags like a dog!

"Daxter"

This is my older half brother, Daxter! He has a different father than us, but he helped raise the three of us when my mom had another litter after him! He can be quite skittish and jumpy, but he's still a very happy kitty.
